VSee Unified Platform Architecture One EMR backend, configurable frontends per deployment mode

VSee Health
Core Concept: VSee EMR is the single source of truth for all patient records — clinical and financial. The EMR has an Online (cloud) and Offline (snapshot) mode that sync bidirectionally — enabling disaster/field deployments to capture data without connectivity and merge back later. Each deployment mode gets a configurable Digital Front Door.
